Generations Genealogy

Rebuilding Families One Story at a Time

Welcome to Generations Genealogy, hosted by Jilleen Walch Phillips.

I have found that as I work through my genealogy research, I am collecting bits of information on lots of families. On this site, I will be sharing what I find, in the hopes that something may be useful to another researcher.

I am also slowly building my skills as a family history researcher. In the spirit of learning in public I will be sharing my notes.

So, pour yourself a cup of tea and spend some time exploring my projects. If you’ve been here before, you might like to check out what’s new. And if you find anything useful, I would be delighted to hear from you.
